The material is the design

is our cashmere.

Most furniture brands treat material as a starting point — then cover it with stain, veneer, or paint. We go the other direction. We chose one material and made it the whole point.

The stacked plies on every edge aren't a side effect — they're the signature. The warm, consistent grain on every face isn't a compromise — it's the finish. Two coats of hand-rubbed oil. That's it.
